## FARMERS (Target: Builder Tier — hardest to recruit)

Strategy: Don't cold-recruit farmers. They come through:
1. **EMILI's Innovation Farm Network** — Russell already manages this (17,000+ acres)
2. **Pilot partnerships** — when a founder on the platform needs a pilot, the farmer joins
3. **Western Growers** — Walt Duflock's network
4. **Farm shows & events** — in-person recruitment with QR codes

The farmer persona is the "demand side" — they come when there's something valuable for them (early tech access, pilot payments, voice in product development).
